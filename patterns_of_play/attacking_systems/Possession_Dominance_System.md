
# SYSTEM 1: “POSSESSION DOMINANCE ENGINE”

### (Positional Play + Combination Play + Third-Man Run Attack)

This is the foundation system of elite control teams.

---

## STRUCTURAL IDEA

Control space first → manipulate pressure → break lines with third-man runs.

---

## PHASE 1: BUILD-UP (POSSESSION PLAY FORM)

### ✔ ATTACKING FORM: **Positional Play (Juego de Posición)**

```text
CB → DM → LCM → LB
```

### Player movements:

* CBs spread wide
* DM drops to form triangle
* LB pushes into midfield line
* LCM occupies half-space

✔ Goal: create **structured superiority**

---

## PHASE 2: CIRCULATION TRAP

### ✔ ATTACKING FORM: **Combination Play**

```text
LCM ↔ DM ↔ RB ↔ RCM
```

### Movements:

* players rotate positions slightly
* one-touch passes attract press
* ball is intentionally “trapped” on one side

✔ Goal: lure opponent into overload

---

## PHASE 3: BREAK PHASE

### ✔ ATTACKING FORM: **Third-Man Run Attack**

```text
DM → LCM → RW (third-man run)
```

### Movements:

* ST drags CB
* LCM bounces pass instantly
* RW runs blindside behind defense

✔ Goal: vertical penetration

---

## FINAL OUTPUT

* cutback OR through ball OR penalty spot finish
