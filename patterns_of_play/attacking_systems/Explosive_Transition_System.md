# SYSTEM 3: “EXPLOSIVE TRANSITION SYSTEM”

### (Fast Breaks + Direct Attack + Isolation Attack)

---

## STRUCTURAL IDEA

Win ball → attack immediately → isolate best attacker.

---

## PHASE 1: BALL WIN

### ✔ ATTACKING FORM: **Fast Breaks**

```text
CM wins ball → ST runs forward instantly
```

### Movements:

* all attackers sprint vertically
* no buildup structure
* maximum tempo

---

## PHASE 2: DIRECT PENETRATION

### ✔ ATTACKING FORM: **Direct Attacks**

```text
CB → ST → LW/RW
```

### Movements:

* first pass forward always
* midfield bypassed
* defense is unsettled

✔ Goal: reach final third in 2–3 passes

---

## PHASE 3: ISOLATION CREATION

### ✔ ATTACKING FORM: **Isolation Attack**

```text
RW isolated 1v1 vs LB
```

### Movements:

* opposite winger stays wide
* CM arrives late
* ST occupies CBs

✔ Goal: create clean 1v1

---

## FINAL OUTPUT

* dribble → shot OR cutback OR 1v1 finish
