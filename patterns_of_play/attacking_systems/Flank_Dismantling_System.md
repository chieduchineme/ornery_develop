
# SYSTEM 2: “FLANK DISMANTLING SYSTEM”

### (Overload Attack + Underlapping Attack + Combination Crossing Attack)

---

## STRUCTURAL IDEA

Destroy one side, rotate defenders, then cross into pre-built box.

---

## PHASE 1: OVERLOAD CREATION

### ✔ ATTACKING FORM: **Overload Attack**

```text
LW + LB + LCM (left overload triangle)
```

### Movements:

* LW isolates fullback
* LB overlaps
* LCM supports inside

✔ Goal: force defensive collapse toward wing

---

## PHASE 2: INTERNAL BREAK

### ✔ ATTACKING FORM: **Underlapping Attack**

```text
LB → inside channel run
```

### Movements:

* LW stays wide (fixes defender)
* LB underlaps into half-space
* LCM feeds inside lane

✔ Goal: break defensive line internally

---

## PHASE 3: DELIVERY BUILD-UP

### ✔ ATTACKING FORM: **Combination Crossing Attack**

```text
LW ↔ LCM ↔ LB → cross
```

### Movements:

* 2–3 passes before cross
* defenders shift laterally
* box structure pre-arranged

✔ Goal: create high-quality crossing lane

---

## FINAL OUTPUT

* cutback OR driven cross into penalty spot
