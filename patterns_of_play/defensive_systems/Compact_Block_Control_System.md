# SYSTEM 2: COMPACT BLOCK CONTROL SYSTEM

### (Mid-Block + Compactness Defending + Funnel Defending)

---

## STRUCTURAL IDEA

Control the opponent without chasing them, then force play into predictable wide zones.

---

## PHASE 1: MIDFIELD SCREEN

### DEFENSIVE FORM: **Mid-Block**

```text
      ST    ST
LM   CM   CM   RM
LB   CB   CB   RB
```

### Movements:

* forwards screen the pivot
* midfield line protects central progression
* back line stays close enough to prevent pockets

---

## PHASE 2: TEAM COMPACTNESS

### DEFENSIVE FORM: **Compactness Defending**

```text
short vertical distance
narrow horizontal distance
```

### Movements:

* whole block shifts toward the ball
* far winger tucks inside
* center-backs communicate the line height

---

## PHASE 3: FORCED DIRECTION

### DEFENSIVE FORM: **Funnel Defending**

```text
inside lane closed -> ball forced wide
```

### Movements:

* presser blocks central lane
* near midfielder protects half-space
* full-back prepares touchline duel

---

## FINAL OUTPUT

* opponent circulates wide, crosses from poor zones, or plays backward
