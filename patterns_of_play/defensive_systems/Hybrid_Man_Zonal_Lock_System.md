# SYSTEM 5: HYBRID MAN-ZONAL LOCK SYSTEM

### (Hybrid Defending + Man-Oriented Pressing + Zonal Defending + Defensive Overload)

---

## STRUCTURAL IDEA

Stay zonal away from the ball, lock opponents near the ball, and overload the danger zone.

---

## PHASE 1: BASE SHAPE

### DEFENSIVE FORM: **Zonal Defending**

```text
team shifts as a connected block
players protect zones away from ball
```

### Movements:

* far-side players stay compact
* back line passes runners across zones
* midfield line protects central lanes

---

## PHASE 2: LOCAL LOCKS

### DEFENSIVE FORM: **Man-Oriented Pressing**

```text
near-side CM -> opponent CM
full-back -> winger
winger -> full-back
```

### Movements:

* near-side defenders follow receivers tightly
* striker screens the center-back return
* spare defender covers behind the duels

---

## PHASE 3: BALL-SIDE SUPERIORITY

### DEFENSIVE FORM: **Defensive Overload**

```text
4 defenders around 3 attackers
```

### Movements:

* wide player is double-teamed
* midfielder blocks half-space
* center-back covers channel

---

## FINAL OUTPUT

* opponent cannot progress locally and is forced backward, wide, or into a contested duel
