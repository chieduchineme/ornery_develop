# SYSTEM 6: HIGH LINE SWEEPER SYSTEM

### (Offside Trap + Goalkeeper Sweeper Defense + High Press + Rest Defense)

---

## STRUCTURAL IDEA

Compress the pitch with a high line, then protect the space behind it with timing and goalkeeper coverage.

---

## PHASE 1: TERRITORIAL PRESSURE

### DEFENSIVE FORM: **High Press**

```text
front line presses
midfield squeezes
back line holds near halfway
```

### Movements:

* attackers force rushed forward passes
* midfielders pressure the passer
* defenders stay brave and compact

---

## PHASE 2: LINE TIMING

### DEFENSIVE FORM: **Offside Trap**

```text
CB calls step
back four move together
runner caught beyond line
```

### Movements:

* center-back leads the step
* full-backs match the timing
* midfield pressure delays the pass

---

## PHASE 3: DEPTH COVER

### DEFENSIVE FORM: **Goalkeeper Sweeper Defense**

```text
GK starts high
through ball attacked early
```

### Movements:

* goalkeeper reads passes behind
* center-backs recover inside
* goalkeeper clears or recycles possession

---

## PHASE 4: POSSESSION SECURITY

### DEFENSIVE FORM: **Rest Defense**

* high line remains connected during attacks
* holding midfielder protects central counters
* goalkeeper supports behind the line

---

## FINAL OUTPUT

* opponent has no easy space in front of the block or behind the back line
