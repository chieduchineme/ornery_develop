# SYSTEM 3: DEEP BOX PROTECTION SYSTEM

### (Low Block + Back Five Defending + Box Defending + Set-Piece Defense)

---

## STRUCTURAL IDEA

Accept territory, protect the penalty area, and win the decisive aerial and second-ball moments.

---

## PHASE 1: DEEP BLOCK

### DEFENSIVE FORM: **Low Block**

```text
        ST
LM   CM   CM   RM
LB   CB   CB   RB
```

### Movements:

* midfield line drops close to defense
* wide midfielders track full-backs
* striker screens easy recycling passes

---

## PHASE 2: WIDTH PROTECTION

### DEFENSIVE FORM: **Back Five Defending**

```text
LWB  LCB  CB  RCB  RWB
```

### Movements:

* wing-back engages crosser
* outside center-back covers channel
* far wing-back protects back post

---

## PHASE 3: PENALTY-AREA CONTROL

### DEFENSIVE FORM: **Box Defending**

```text
near post
central striker
far post
cutback
edge
```

### Movements:

* center-backs attack first contact
* midfielders track cutbacks
* edge defender contests rebounds

---

## PHASE 4: DEAD-BALL CONTINUITY

### DEFENSIVE FORM: **Set-Piece Defense**

* same box responsibilities carry into corners and wide free kicks
* strongest aerial players protect central zones
* outlet player prepares the escape

---

## FINAL OUTPUT

* blocked shots, cleared crosses, controlled second balls, and lower-quality opponent chances
