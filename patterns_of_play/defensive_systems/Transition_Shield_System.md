# SYSTEM 4: TRANSITION SHIELD SYSTEM

### (Rest Defense + Counter-Press + Transition Defense)

---

## STRUCTURAL IDEA

Attack with numbers while keeping enough defensive structure to kill counter-attacks immediately.

---

## PHASE 1: BEHIND-BALL SECURITY

### DEFENSIVE FORM: **Rest Defense**

```text
attack ahead of ball
2-3 or 3-2 structure behind
GK ready to sweep
```

### Movements:

* center-backs stay connected
* holding midfielder protects central lane
* far-side full-back tucks inside when needed

---

## PHASE 2: IMMEDIATE RECOVERY

### DEFENSIVE FORM: **Counter-Press**

```text
loss -> nearest player presses
support players block exits
```

### Movements:

* first defender attacks the ball
* second defender blocks the forward pass
* third defender protects the inside lane

---

## PHASE 3: FALLBACK DECISION

### DEFENSIVE FORM: **Transition Defense**

```text
if counter-press fails -> delay -> recover shape
```

### Movements:

* defenders drop if no pressure exists
* midfielders sprint through central lanes
* wide players recover toward the middle first

---

## FINAL OUTPUT

* counter stopped early OR opponent slowed long enough for the block to reset
