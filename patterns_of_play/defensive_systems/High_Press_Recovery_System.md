# SYSTEM 1: HIGH PRESS RECOVERY SYSTEM

### (High Press + Cover Shadow Defending + Pressing Traps)

---

## STRUCTURAL IDEA

Force the opponent to build where you want, then recover the ball near goal.

---

## PHASE 1: FIRST LINE PRESS

### DEFENSIVE FORM: **High Press**

```text
ST curves run at CB
LW/RW lock full-backs
CMs ready to jump
```

### Movements:

* striker blocks the switch between center-backs
* winger presses full-back from outside to inside
* midfield line squeezes behind the front three

---

## PHASE 2: LANE CONTROL

### DEFENSIVE FORM: **Cover Shadow Defending**

```text
ST presses CB while hiding DM
```

### Movements:

* first presser uses curved body shape
* midfielders lock the visible receivers
* back line holds high to compress the pitch

---

## PHASE 3: TRAP EXECUTION

### DEFENSIVE FORM: **Pressing Traps**

```text
CB -> FB
winger + CM + full-back collapse
```

### Movements:

* full-back pass is intentionally invited
* nearest three defenders close exits
* far-side players protect the switch

---

## FINAL OUTPUT

* turnover near the box OR rushed clearance OR immediate attacking transition
